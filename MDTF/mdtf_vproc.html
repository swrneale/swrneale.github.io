ENSO Vertical Processes Diagnostic Package

This MDTF Process Oriented Diagnostics is designed to compliment and inform the mean and seasonal anomalies of diveregnce source terms in the Rossby Wave Source POD. It produces a vertical profiles of the divergent and other column quantities essential to our understanding of vertical tendency processes in GCMs and how they relate to parameterized processes.

POD Documentation

Analysis regions:
  • TCP: Tropical Centeral Pacific 160E-140W; 10S-EQ (Main tropical forcing)
  • STP: Sub-tropical Pacific 150E-160W; 25-40N (RWS generation region)
  • TWP: Tropical West Pacific 110E-150E; EQ-15N (Additional contribution to RWS)
